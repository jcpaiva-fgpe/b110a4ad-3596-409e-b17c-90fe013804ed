In Europe, the following denominations are in circulation: 1 cent, 2 cent, 5 cent, 10 cent, 20 cent, 50 cent, 1 Euro, 2 Euro, 5 Euro, 10 Euro, 20 Euro, 50 Euro, 100 Euro, 200 Euro, 500 Euro. The value of the acquired products bill and the customer's payment cash are inputs to the program.

The program has to calculate how much money is left after completing the payment: r = cash - bill and display it. Apply also some code that figures out the combination of denominations for the smallest feasible amount of coins and banknotes issued, show them to the user in form as below.


Consider the following scenario:


bill = 171,24

cash = 200


Output:

(r = 28.76 Euro) 20 Euro, 5 Euro, 2 Euro, 1 Euro, 0.5 Euro, 0.2 Euro, 0.05 Euro, 0.01 Euro
